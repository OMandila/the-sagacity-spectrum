FSC Forest Atlas, Product Lead, 03/2023-08/2023

FSC Members voted to make it mandatory for FSC International to collect geolocation data from all FSC Certified forests globally. This required FSC to develop a platform for collection and proactive management of forest data submitted by forest managers worldwide.

Through rapid prototyping, leading a team of GIS developers and Data Analyst, we created and integrated the new FSC Forest Atlas with existing FSC IT systems for user authentication and data management.

  • A fully-functioning ArcGIS Hub application minimum viable product (MVP).
  • A data-driven early adopter program to get initial forest managers onboard.
H12023

Data Collection & Analytics: Shared Values in Plantation Forests, Digitization Lead, 11/2022-11/2023

The Sustainable Intensification Advisory Group of FSC International commissioned Form International consultants to develop a system for data collection to identify shared values in Plantation Forests in South Africa, New Zealand and Australia.

Working with the project team, I led the transformation of the questionnaire into a mobile-based data collection form.

I led the integration of the form with analytics reports as well as ensured GDPR-compliance in the design for the data collection tool.

I led the user training and ensure users were comfortable adopting and utilizing the platform, both at data entry and analytics/reporting.

  • Superior data collection and data integration was achieved.
  • User experience was multiple fold better than on MS Excel.
  • Automated data management and analytics meant faster time-to-value for the data.
  • Possibility to monitor and evaluate users experience on the tool.
