FSC Data Strategy & Data Architec' Design, 06/2023-03/2024, Project Lead

FSC needed to modernize its data architecture to enhance data utilization in risk management, monitoring & evaluation, marketing, communication, and innovation, focusing on efficient data collection, processing, storage, retrieval, cataloging, classification, and enabling self-service analytics and visualization.

I led the recruitment of new data and GIS experts into the FSC data team. I managed strategic collaborations with:

  • ISEAL Data Community of Practice to leverage the ISEAL Data Maturity Rubric.
  • Esri to leverage the Esri ArcGIS Suite of data management software.
  • Microsoft to leverage the Intelligent Data Platform implementation framework.

Under my leadership, the FSC data team worked on the following data & analytics initiatives:

  1. The FSC Data & Innovations Program Charter:- A program leadership and business continuity document describing the scope, governance and objectives of the program in relation to business needs and desired strategic benefits.
  2. The FSC-Esri Enterprise Agreement:- A business case containing cost-benefits analysis for the FSC leadership team to consider a strategic partnership with Esri in order to access and deploy Esri's cutting-edge geolocation data management software technologies like the ArcGIS Suite.
  3. The FSC Check:- A new platform for FSC to conduct due diligence checks on new certification clients.
  4. Shared Value from FSC Certified Plantation forests: South Africa, New Zealand, and Australia Pilot:- A Mobile Data Collection & Analytics tool for FSC certified forest managers who want to quantify the shared value of their forests in their ecosystem.
  5. StoryMaps based strategic communications tool:- The media and communications team working with the data team created interactive and immersive stories based on ArcGIS StoryMaps technology to showcase the value of FSC Certification.
  6. The FSC Forest Atlas:- A GIS platform meant to collect, visualize and manage forest boundary data from all FSC Certified Forests globally.
  7. The Intelligent Data Platform:- A new data management and data governance platform built on Microsoft Fabric to enable better data collection, data access, and data use by FSC stakeholders.

FSC Forest Atlas, Product Lead, 03/2023-08/2023

FSC Members voted to make it mandatory for FSC International to collect geolocation data from all FSC Certified forests globally. This required FSC to develop a platform for collection and proactive management of forest data submitted by forest managers worldwide.

Through rapid prototyping, leading a team of GIS developers and Data Analyst, we created and integrated the new FSC Forest Atlas with existing FSC IT systems for user authentication and data management.

  • A fully-functioning ArcGIS Hub application minimum viable product (MVP).
  • A data-driven early adopter program to get initial forest managers onboard.

Data Collection & Analytics: Shared Values in Plantation Forests, Digitization Lead, 11/2022-11/2023

The Sustainable Intensification Advisory Group of FSC International commissioned Form International consultants to develop a system for data collection to identify shared values in Plantation Forests in South Africa, New Zealand and Australia.

Working with the project team, I led the transformation of the questionnaire into a mobile-based data collection form.

I led the integration of the form with analytics reports as well as ensured GDPR-compliance in the design for the data collection tool.

I led the user training and ensured users were comfortable adopting and utilizing the platform, both at data entry and analytics/reporting.

  • Superior data collection and data integration was achieved.
  • User experience was multiple fold better than on MS Excel.
  • Automated data management and analytics meant faster time-to-value for the data.
  • Possibility to monitor and evaluate users experience on the tool.

FSC Data-driven Risk Management, 04/2023-12/2023

FSC sought to develop an automated model for predicting the risk of forced-terminations for both current and new certificate holders. The pilot solution of this data model was developed using only FSC's internal datasets.

I provided technical support and insights in the project design, procurement & contracting of Stats4SD as a technology partner, project implementation, and deployment of the R-based statistical data model of the Forced-Terminations Risk Prediction Model.

This project led to the creation of the first data-driven risk management model that put FSC Data Assets to a high-value internal use-case.

FSC Check, 11/2022-12/2023

USAID-funded Transition to New Sudan Programme: Sudan Civil Service Reforms Assessment, Data Manager, 08/2021–10/2021

The PwC team on the project needed to collect quantitative and qualitative data from approx. 5,000 civil servants in the civil service of Sudan's Transition Government.

  • Working with the project team, I transformed the questionnaire into a mobile-based data collection form
  • I led data management, analytics, visualization, and dissemination of insights that guided the project recommendations and final report development.

The project team ultimately collected critical project data and managed user consent for more than 3,700 civil servants, both in the central and federal government levels.
